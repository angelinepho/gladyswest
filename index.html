Meet Gladys West,

the "hidden figure" behind GPS

Gladys is an American mathematician who is known for her contributions to the mathematical modeling of the shape of the Earth. She also helped develop the satellite geodesy models that were eventually incorporated into the Global Positioning System (GPS).

In 2018, Gladys was inducted into the United States Air Force Hall of Fame. She has left a remarkable legacy and will continue to inspire girls and people of color to pursue careers in STEM!

Thank you Gladys West!
